This Web-App JavaScript Calculator was made in mid 2024. It is an eight digit Integer calculator, that will help you do some simple mathematical operations.

Those operations include:

  • Addition
  • Subtraction
  • Division
  • Multiplication
  • Modulo
  • Exponent

There's also a "CLS button (Clear Screen) that removes any numbers and calculations and resets the calculator to its initial state.
