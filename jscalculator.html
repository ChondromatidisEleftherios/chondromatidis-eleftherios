Rock-Paper-Scissors is a Browser Console Game made in 2023. The player goes 5 rounds against the Computer. The player types its choice on the top of the browser window, and he can view the round results from the browser's console, like shown in the images down below.

After all 5 rounds are over, the winner will be declared by the amount of their points. Trust me, the Computer won't go easy on you, so Good Luck!
